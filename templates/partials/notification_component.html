iOS Users: Important Notice

For notifications to work properly on your iPhone/iPad, you must:

  1. Tap the share icon at the bottom of Safari
  2. Select "Add to Home Screen" and tap "Add"
  3. Close Safari completely (swipe up from bottom)
  4. Open the app from your home screen icon
  5. Then enable notifications when prompted

Web push notifications on iOS only work when launched from the home screen.
