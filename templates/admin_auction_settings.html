About Minimum Balance Requirements

The minimum balance requirement ensures teams can participate in all remaining rounds of the auction.

Example Calculation:

  • 15 rounds completed, 10 rounds remain
  • Minimum balance requirement: 30 per round
  • Required team balance: 30 × 10 = 300

This helps ensure fair competition throughout the entire auction process and prevents teams from being unable to participate in later rounds.
