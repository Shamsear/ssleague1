What's New

Version 2.0.0 | May 30, 2023

Onboarding Tutorial Added

New users will now be guided through a comprehensive interactive tutorial explaining how the auction system works. The tutorial covers bidding, tiebreakers, and team management.

You can access the tutorial anytime from the help menu in the top navigation bar.

Notification Center

Stay informed with our new notification center! Track bid statuses, auction updates, and important events all in one place. Notifications are categorized by type and you can mark them as read or important.

Access the notification center by clicking the bell icon in the top navigation bar.

Interactive Tooltips

We've added helpful tooltips throughout the interface to explain complex features and interface elements. Hover over or focus on elements with a dotted underline to see detailed explanations.

Accessibility Improvements

We've made significant accessibility improvements throughout the site, including:

  • Proper ARIA labels on all interactive elements
  • Enhanced color contrast ratios for better readability
  • Improved keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility with semantic HTML
  • Focus indicators for keyboard users

Our goal is to make the platform accessible to all users, regardless of ability.

"Remember Me" Login Functionality

Stay logged in securely for longer periods with our new "Remember Me" feature. This uses secure token-based authentication to keep you signed in for 30 days, even after closing your browser.

Your security is our priority - tokens are stored securely and encrypted.
